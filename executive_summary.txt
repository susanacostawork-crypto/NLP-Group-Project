Executive Summary:

The analysis of 337 Google reviews reveals a mixed sentiment towards Majestic Café in Porto, with a slight leaning towards negative opinions. While 43% of reviews are positive, 53% are negative, indicating a need for improvement. Customers appreciate the café's ambience and service, with 51% of mentions for these aspects being positive. The strongest aspect is the café's unique atmosphere, which draws customers in and leaves a lasting impression.

However, the main complaint is the high prices, with 63% of price-related mentions being negative. Customers feel that the prices are excessive, making the café a "tourist trap." To address this, we recommend revising the pricing strategy to offer more competitive and transparent options, such as introducing a tiered pricing system or offering value-for-money sets. By doing so, Majestic Café can balance its commercial goals with customer expectations, ultimately enhancing the overall experience and improving customer satisfaction.
